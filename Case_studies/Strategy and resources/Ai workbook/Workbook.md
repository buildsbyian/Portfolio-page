AI Development Workbook
The Problem
Most non-technical people treat AI as a smarter search engine. ChatGPT is the ceiling. The actual capability gap between "chatting with AI" and "shipping a software product using AI" is enormous -- and there was nothing written for operators, founders, or executives that bridged it in plain language.
What I Built
A 19-page PDF manual covering end-to-end AI-assisted software development for people with zero technical background. Covers tool selection, workflow, prompting strategy, decision points, and real examples drawn from my own builds. Written to be followed, not just read.
